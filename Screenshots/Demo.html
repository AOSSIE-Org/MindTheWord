Mind the Word - Comment it works

"Mind The Word" aide you to apprendre vocabulary of a new langue while browsing pages in your native language.

In every webpage visited, it randomly translates a few words en the langue that you would like to learn. By exposing you to only a few new words at a temps and keeping them within context, it makes it facile for you to infer and memorize leur meaning.
